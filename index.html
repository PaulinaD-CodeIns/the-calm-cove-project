What we offer

To help you find your inner zen we currently offer 3 types of yoga that will support you on your journey of personal development with energy healing, breathwork and holistic freeing movement. We offer:

  • Beginners Yoga - Beginners Yoga – New to yoga or returning after a break? This class is a perfect way to ease in, relax, and connect with your practice.
  • The Restorative Yoga - Sink into deep relaxation with passive poses and cozy props (we provide everything). Let go of stress and unwind completely.
  • Yoga Flow - A dynamic, breath-guided flow that’s always fresh and exciting. Our teachers keep it creative and fun, offering something new each time!.
